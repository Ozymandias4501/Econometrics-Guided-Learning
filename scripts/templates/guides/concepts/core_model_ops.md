### Core Model Ops: Reproducibility and Artifacts

This part turns your notebook work into reproducible runs.

#### Key terms (defined)
> **Definition:** An **artifact** is a saved output (model file, metrics JSON, predictions CSV).

> **Definition:** A **run ID** is a unique identifier for a training run.

> **Definition:** A **config** records your modeling choices so results can be reproduced.

#### Why this matters
If you cannot reproduce a result, it is not trustworthy.
